The NUUZ Standard

Redefining Equine Performance Through Digital Precision

Founded at the intersection of bio-mechanical engineering and traditional horsemanship, NUUZ is committed to extending the athletic careers of horses worldwide.

Our journey began with a simple question: How can we use additive manufacturing to solve complex orthopedic challenges? Today, NUUZ operates as a high-tech design hub, leveraging proprietary 3D scanning workflows and Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) to deliver horseshoes that are as unique as the animals that wear them.

Technological Innovation
